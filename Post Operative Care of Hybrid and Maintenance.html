Patient Instructions
POST OPERATIVE CARE OF HYBRID AND MAINTAINANCE

YOU WILL BE ON A SOFT DIET FOR 3-4 MONTHS AS IMPLANTS ARE HEALING

Treat your dental implants like you would take care of your natural teeth.

Part 1 Acceptable Foods to Eat

Fruits/Vegetables

DO NOT – bite or chew on any hard stuff. Cut them into pieces and have it.

DO NOT – crush ice

DO NOT – eat nuts

CAN EAT –

  • Apple Sauce
  • Cooked Fruits or Ripe Fruits
  • Canned fruits without skin or seeds
  • Well-cooked soft vegetables

Meats and Other Proteins

They have to be very tender and not chewy

  • Broiled Fish
  • Roasted/ Stewed Chicken
  • Soups with small soft pieces of vegetables and meat
  • Tofu

Breads/Pasta/Rice

  • Soft loaf bread – cut them into pieces and have it
  • Pancakes/Muffins/Waffles
  • Macaroni/Rice Pasta/Pasta
  • Oatmeal/ Cooked Cereal/ Cream of Wheat
  • Mashed Potatoes

Other

  • Pudding/Jell-O/ Yogurt/ Ice Cream/Protein Shakes
  • Hamburger, Pizza, Bagel – cut into small pieces and have it
Part 2 Oral Hygiene Care
  • You can brush your teeth with the gum part of your hybrid very gently with any paste from the next morning.
  • You can use a manual or electric toothbrush, whichever is convenient for you.
  • We recommend usage of Oral-B electric round toothbrush – Its good for cleaning hybrids.
  • Start using a WATERPIK AFTER ONE WEEK OF RECEIVING YOUR HYBRID BRIDGE.
  • Use the waterpik under your bridges with very slow pressure by lifting your lip and cheek with slightly closed mouth.
  • Cleaning under the hybrid is more necessary to clean than the original teeth.
  • Keep in mind if you don’t waterpik, food will accumulate under your bridges causing plaque buildup and tarter on your implants and abutments.
  • Use your waterpik after each meal, if possible if not make sure to use it at nighttime before bed.
  • Other accessories to clean under the hybrid after one month of gum healing – you can use interdental angled brushes – Tepe Brushes
  • Super Floss if that is convenient for you to do.
Part 3 Maintenance Schedule

1st hybrid cleaning will be after 6 months of hybrid delivery!

Keep in mind that you should get the periodic cleaning and maintenance done by experienced dentist who is familiar with ALL-ON-4 complete implant solution techniques. An ideal maintenance schedule would be twice a year or a thorough cleaning of bridges. It ensures that they last long, and it also provides the stability of your implants.
